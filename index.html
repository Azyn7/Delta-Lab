Welcome to Whiterun

For the first time on your adventure, from your travels from Solstheim unto Cyrodill you have entered into the great province of Skyrim, and into it the city of Whiterun. At first, you're unimpressed. The city is, while lively and green, ultimately rather drab in comparison to the cities farther west and father north, such as Riften and Windhelm.

As you enter, coming from your front is a single guard, although various lay scattered throughout the holds pathways, catching your eye at every turn.

Turning to you, the guard calls out, "You! You're new here. I've never seen your face before." He glanced you up and down, examining your armor, weapons, and belongings, as well as your face and body as a whole, before asking: "What is your name, stranger?"
